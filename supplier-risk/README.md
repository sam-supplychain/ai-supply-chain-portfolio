# Supplier Risk & Procurement Analytics

## Purpose
Supplier risk analytics identifies vulnerabilities in supply continuity, cost, compliance, and quality. It supports proactive sourcing and procurement to prevent disruption.

## Key Concepts
- Supplier performance scorecards
- Risk scoring (financial, geographic, ESG)
- Dual sourcing and safety stock
- Lead time variability and reliability

## Business Decisions Supported
- Supplier selection and evaluation
- Contracting and negotiation strategies
- Risk mitigation (alternate suppliers)
- Procurement planning and compliance

## Expected Outcomes
- Reduced supply disruption risk
- Improved supplier performance
- Stronger cost and contract governance
